Welcome Letter

On behalf of the organizing team, I am ecstatic to welcome you to the first session of Pakistan Embassy International Study Group Model United Nations (PEISGMUN) from 24 to 26 July as the Secretary-General. Even though I would rather have our first session in person, health is our priority as it must be. However, we did not let this demoralize us. We, along with my Director-General Ilhan and Deputy Secretary-General Kiyan, have always wanted to organize our own conference, and thought we would do so during this time where we all are at the comfort of our homes. Our primary aim with our conference is to bring joy to our delegates in these times, and we hope to do so with our selection of committees. For three of our five committees (UNSC, UNHRC, and UNEP), we chose topics that affect the world today in order to shine a light on them and for the delegates to come up with creative and effective solutions. As the other two committees occur in different periods of times (one historical and one futuristic), we chose topics we would love to be delegates in. Things do not have to go according to the way they did in real life, so this way the delegates can have their own say in these events, and shape the future they will face. We have an amazing crisis team, so I can assure you that there will be a lot of action going on. We, as the organizing team of PEISGMUN hope that you will have immense amounts of fun and enjoy our conference. We hope that we can hold a physical conference as soon as safety is restored. I am looking forward to seeing you at PEISGMUN at the end of July and do not forget- we all have the power to shape our own future, so we must use it wisely.

Director General
