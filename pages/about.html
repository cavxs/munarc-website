About our Ari School

MUNARC Is an online conference held from 24 to 26 july, there are 5 committees for you to choose from; Each of these committees have been put together for your enjoyment in these 3 days. Our committees are both interesting and most are beginner friendly, the crisis committees might be a challenge but don’t let that discourage you from applying to these committees, if you do find it difficult your Under Secretary General or your chairs will be able to help you, Our team consists of very specialized in the field and are very kind people like all of us should be, who will help you when you are confused and in need of a helping hand. This conference will be held on zoom except for Historical Crisis Committee which will be held on discord. Our team consists of very specialized and kind people who will help you when you are confused. And yes our conference will give out awards to those who earned it and certificates to everyone who have attended our online Model United Nations This is the very first and official conference of PEISGMUN20, We hope you will like it and will come to the future ones, we will be extremely glad to see you again.
