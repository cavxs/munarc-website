Code of Conduct

  • Language.The official language of our conference is English. Using any other language is strictly out of order.
  • Dress Code.All participants should be aware that in case of missing 2 or more sessions without a proper reason, such as reasons related to health, a certificate will not be provided to the delegates.
  • Allocations.Delegates’ webcams should be open at all times. If there is a reason as to why you cannot open it, notifying the chair-board is necessary.
  • Attendance.If a delegate has to leave a session, they need to notify the chair-board or the organizing team before they leave.
  • Cancellation.All participants must follow the instructions of their chair-board and the organizing team at all costs.
  • Usage of CamerasAll participants have to follow a formal dress code.
  • Allowance of Committee VisitsAll participants should be aware that the organization team and/or their chair-board can record or capture images and/or videos that may be published on the Instagram account of PEISGMUN or used in the closing video.
  • ProhibitionThe use of electronic devices during the session other than the device you are using to join the sessions is forbidden, unless allowed by the chair-board.
